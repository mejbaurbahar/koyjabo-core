TL;DR — KoyJabo (কই যাবো)

KoyJabo is Bangladesh's free offline transport guide covering 200+ Dhaka bus routes, Metro Rail MRT-6 fares (৳20–100, 17 stations), intercity bus/train/flight/launch routes across all 64 districts, truck & freight booking with real BD market rates, a BRTA-rate fare calculator, and a bilingual Bengali/English AI assistant. Works offline.
