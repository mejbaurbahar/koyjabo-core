## 🎯 Ad Placement Strategy by Page

### Content Pages (About, Why Use, FAQ)
**Google's Auto Ads will:**
- Place 1-2 ads per scroll depth
- Use native format for better engagement
- Match content style
- Avoid disrupting reading flow

### Legal Pages (Privacy, Terms)
**Google's Auto Ads will:**
- Place sidebar ads (desktop)
- Top/bottom banners
- Minimal in-content ads
- Respect legal text flow

### App Pages (Home, Bus Details, Live Nav)

- Avoid blocking interactive elements
- Use anchor ads (dismissible)
- Strategic sidebar placement
- Native ads in result lists
