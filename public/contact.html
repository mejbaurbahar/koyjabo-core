Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report incorrect bus route information?

Email us at mejbaurbaharfagun@gmail.com with:

  • Bus name/number
  • What's incorrect (route, fare, stops, etc.)
  • Correct information (if known)

We'll verify and update within 1-2 business days.

Can I contribute new bus routes?

Absolutely! We welcome community contributions. You can:

  • Email us the route details
  • Open a pull request on GitHub (for developers)
  • Submit an issue with the route information

All contributions will be credited!

How do I install KoyJabo as an app?

KoyJabo is a Progressive Web App (PWA). To install:

  • Android/Chrome: Click the "Add to Home Screen" prompt when you visit koyjabo.com
  • iOS/Safari: Tap Share → Add to Home Screen
  • Desktop: Click the install icon in your browser's address bar

No app store needed - it works on all devices!

Does KoyJabo work offline?

Yes! Once you've visited KoyJabo, all route data is cached locally on your device. You can search for routes even without an internet connection. Previously searched routes remain accessible offline indefinitely.

Is KoyJabo really free?

100% free! We display minimal, non-intrusive ads (via Google AdSense) to cover hosting costs, but the core functionality is completely free with no hidden charges, subscriptions, or paywalls.

Can I use KoyJabo data for my project?

For non-commercial, educational, or research purposes - yes! Please credit KoyJabo and link back to koyjabo.com. For commercial use, please contact us for licensing.

Why isn't my city/district covered?

We're currently focused on Dhaka metro and major intercity routes. Expansion to other cities (Chittagong, Sylhet, Rajshahi, etc.) is planned for 2026-2027. Stay tuned!
